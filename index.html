About Us

Tunas Mitra is a packaging supplier company that was established in 1999 under the name TM Supply. As time goes by, we continue to grow and have expanded into a company that has branches in several major cities in Indonesia.

We provide various packaging needs such as OPP Tape, Stretch Film, Strapping Band, Packaging Machine, and various other supporting packaging materials.

Our Products

OPP Tape

Lakban or OPP Tape is a combination of BOPP film coated with acrylic water-based adhesive for various applications. Tura Tape offers the best quality adhesive and provides instant bonding to products, making it suitable for light, medium, and heavy-duty packaging. It has been widely used in various industrial sectors.

Stretch Film

Stretch Film is made from Linear Low-Density Polyethylene (LLDPE), a highly elastic plastic material that tightly wraps around products. Generally, plastic wrapping is used to wrap products on pallets to protect them from dust, water, and other contaminants. The use of plastic wrapping helps reduce the risk of product damage and unwanted workplace accidents.

Strapping Band

PP (PolyPropylene) strapping bands are commonly used to tie cardboard boxes and pallets to maintain product stability during storage and transportation. Almost all business fields use PP strapping bands, both national and multi-national companies that use strapping bands to export their products.

Stretch Film Machine

The One Wrap Stretch Wrapping Machine is the perfect, fast, and optimal solution for the most commonly used pallet wrapping needs in companies. With modern technology, this machine has the main function of wrapping cardboard boxes with stretch film to protect them from dust, water, and dirt during storage and shipping.
